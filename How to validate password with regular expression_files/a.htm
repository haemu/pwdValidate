                            /^(?=.*\d)(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])[a-zA-Z\d@#$%_-]{8,20}$/

                            required; min 1 lowercase letter, min 1 uppercase letter, @#$%_- special character accepting. disallow spaces, minlength 8 maxlength 20 character.

                            good luck.
